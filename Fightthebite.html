Fight the Bite

A dengue awareness program was organized by the Lions Club of Nawala to join the Worldwide Week of Service dedicated to mentoring, empowering and engaging youth through community service. This international event was designed to inspire the next generation of volunteers and help Lions to reach the Centennial Service Challenge goal of serving 100+ million people. In collaboration with the Lions Club of Nawala, Leo Club of Nawala Metro, Leo Club of Mahanama, Leo Club of Ananda Collee, Leo Club of Carey College and Leo Club of Thurstan College carried out an awareness programme in and around the city of Nugegoda making community aware about dengue and measures to prevent dengue epidemiology.
