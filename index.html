Welcome to

Leo Club of Nawala Metro

Leo Club of Nawala Metro is a relative new comer to the scene. It was installed by Leo District 306 C2 Sri Lanka and sponsored by the Lions Club of Nawala in the year 2013. It is a collaborative effort of young men and women from different social, cultural, academic and professional backgrounds. From our inception we have been able to render tremendous service to the community by being engaged in a wide variety of community Service and other Social Service work. Apart from the good work towards the betterment of the community,

we are also well known for our innovativeness and the passion to do things our way. As a result of hard work, dedication and out of the box attitude towards serving the country we were able to achieve the titles of Most Outstanding Leo Club in Sri Lanka in the year 2013/2014 and Most Outstanding Leo Club in Sri Lanka in the year 2014/2015. The incredible support and guidance we receive from our parent Lions Club and our Leo Advisor, Lion Buddhika Weerasekara is a great strength for us to work passionately towards the needy.

Be updated! Leo Club of Nawala Metro is recognized as an innovative Leo Club in the island.
